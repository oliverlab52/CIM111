Museums

There are two popular museums in Coral Gables. The first being the Coral Gables Museum which opened it's doors in November of 2011. The next is the Lowe Art Museum located on the University of Miami campus.

Transportation

Each day approximately 5,000 riders enjoy Coral Gables' trolley system that was introduced in 2003.

University of Miami

Coral Gables is the home to the University of Miami, the top private university in Florida.

Sports

Coral Gables is home to the Watsco Center which is the home of the Hurricanes men's and women's basketball teams. Also, Marklight Field and Cobb Stadium which are home to the Hurricane's baseball and soccer teams respectively.
